What do we do?

At the beginning of each year, the American Mock Trial Association releases new case materials. We spend the rest of the year debating the same case with other schools around the country.

Debate

Join the team as an attorney to hone your legal skills. Attorneys win points for the team by making speeches, examining witnesses, and arguing objections.

Act

Join the team as a witness to work on your improv. Witnesses win points by being likeable and realistic. Greiving widows should be sad, and theme park mascots should be funny.

Compete

Test what you've prepared against some of the best mock trial teams in the country. Our rounds are judged by practicing judges and attorneys in real courthouses.

Win

We regularly place first or second in the region. Join the team, work hard, and wait for the fan mail to roll in.
